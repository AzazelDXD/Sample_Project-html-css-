My Collection

Collecting and preserving leaves is a great way to learn tree identification.
It is also a method that can be used to document the different varieties of trees on your property or to inventory trees at a park or school campus as a class project.

Name Type Description
Simple A North American native, red maple trees form an oval crown of bright green leaves that turn fiery red in autumn.
Compound A clover is a small plant with bright green leaves and white or purple flowers.
Palmately Compound The American chestnut has long, canoe-shaped leaves with a prominent lance-shaped tip and hooked teeth around the edges of the leaf. The leaf is dull or “matte” instead of shiny or waxy in texture.
